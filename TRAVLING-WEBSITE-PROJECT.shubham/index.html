"The Legend of Surkanda Mata Temple"

The legend of the Surkanda Mata temple holds great significance. According to the Puranas, during a celestial celebration of the marriage of Lord Shiva and Parvati Devi, all the gods and goddesses were present. However, an asura named Shankhchud arrogantly insisted on being present in his disguised form. Shankhchud had promised at that time to reveal the emperors. In order to stop him, Parvati Mata attempted to fall from the sky in her heavy-bodied form. However, when her body fell from the sky, it was so heavy that the Earth said it could not hold it. Then Mata Parvati squeezed Shankhchud with both her hands and pressed him down on the Earth. As a result, her name became Surkanda, and she appeared as Surkanda. Since then, this place testifies to the glory of the mother and preserves this temple in tradition.

"Spiritual Significance of Surkanda Mata Temple"

The religious significance of the Surkanda Mata temple is profound. The worship of Mother Surkanda is done with great reverence here. Devotees experience the grace of God here. The beauty of the surrounding forests and mountains attracts devotees. The religious significance of the Surkanda Mata temple is also documented in folklore, scriptures, and ancient texts. Various religious and cultural programs are organized here, in which devotees actively participate. Understanding the story and significance of the temple, devotees become immersed in their worship and devotion. Every year, many people come to Surkanda Mata for darshan and worship. The sacred atmosphere and presence of the goddess bring peace and joy to people. Being a pilgrimage site, Surkanda Mata temple has become a center for spiritual experiences for devotees.
